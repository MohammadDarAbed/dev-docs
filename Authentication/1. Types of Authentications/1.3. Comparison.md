## 🛡️ Why is Bearer (Token-Based) Authentication Safer than Basic?

|Feature|Basic Authentication|Bearer Token (JWT)|
|---|---|---|
|🔑 Authentication method|Username and password sent on every request|Temporary token sent (not credentials)|
|🔒 Password exposure risk|High (credentials sent every time)|Low (password sent only once)|
|⏳ Expiration|No built-in expiration|Built-in token expiration|
|🔁 Token renewal|Not supported|Supported via refresh tokens|
|🔐 Encryption/Signing|Not built-in|JWTs can be signed or encrypted|

> 💡 With Bearer tokens (especially JWT), even if someone steals a token, its lifetime is limited, and refresh tokens can be revoked.
